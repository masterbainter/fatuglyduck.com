Enter the Big Top

Invasion Timeline

20:00 - THE DESCENT

Arrival in Crescent Cove

A glowing meteor streaks across the sky, landing in the woods. Mike Tobacco and Debbie Stone go to investigate, expecting a comet but finding a glowing circus tent instead.

21:30 - THE FIRST HARVEST

The Farmers Trap

Farmer Gene Greene is the first to encounter the visitors. Mistaking them for pranksters, he is quickly captured in a cocoon of glowing cotton candy.

23:00 - TOWN TERROR

Main Street Massacre

The Klowns enter the town proper. Popcorn guns, balloon hounds, and shadow puppets are used to eliminate the local populace while the Terenzi brothers are busy with their ice cream truck.

01:00 - THE SHOWDOWN

Assault on the Big Top

Dave Hanson leads the rescue mission into the Klown Ship to save Debbie. The true scale of the harvest is revealed: a warehouse full of cotton candy cocoons.

02:30 - FINALE

Klownzilla Awakens

The massive Klownzilla is unleashed. Only the piercing of the red nose can stop the nightmare. The ship explodes, and the town is left in pieces.

The Cast

M

Mike Tobacco

THE HERO

The first to see the ship. He spends the night trying to convince the cynical police that Crescent Cove is under attack by aliens.

D

Dave Hanson

THE SKEPTIC COP

A young deputy who initially laughs off the report until he sees the cotton candy cocoons for himself. Eventually saves the day.

T

Terenzi Brothers

THE COMIC RELIEF

Owners of the "Jojo's Ice Cream" truck. Their loud PA system and absurd outfits make them the perfect distraction for the Klowns.
